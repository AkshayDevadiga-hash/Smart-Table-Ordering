Our story

Saffron House was born out of a simple belief — that great food is a form of hospitality. Founded in 2012 by the Mehta family in the heart of Indiranagar, we started as a 20-seat neighbourhood eatery and grew into one of the city's most loved dining destinations. Our kitchen celebrates the diverse culinary heritage of India, from slow-cooked Awadhi biryanis to coastal seafood curries, brought together under one roof with warmth and care.

Meet the chef

Chef Arjun Rao
Executive Chef & Culinary Director
With over 18 years of experience across Mumbai, Dubai, and London, Chef Arjun returned home to Bengaluru with a mission — to reintroduce India to its own forgotten recipes. Trained at the Culinary Institute of America, he brings classical technique to deeply traditional cooking. He believes the best ingredient is always patience.
