About TableOrder

A modern QR-code-based ordering system that streamlines the dining experience — from menu browsing and order placement to kitchen management and payment.

What We Do

TableOrder replaces printed menus and verbal order-taking with a seamless digital flow. Customers scan a QR code on their table, browse the menu, add items to their cart, and place an order — all from their own phone. Kitchen staff receive orders instantly on a live dashboard, and customers can track status in real time.

Key Features

📱
QR Code Ordering
Scan the table QR code to instantly access the digital menu — no app download needed.
⏱️
Live Order Tracking
Customers see real-time status updates from Pending through Preparing to Delivered.
👨‍🍳
Kitchen Dashboard
Kitchen staff manage orders on a live kanban board with one-tap status updates.
🛒
Smart Cart
Cart persists across page refreshes. GST calculated automatically at 18%.
📊
Admin Dashboard
Revenue stats, popular items, and detailed income reports by day, week, month, or year.
⭐
Customer Reviews
Customers can leave a star rating and comment after their order is delivered or paid.
🖼️
Menu with Images
Admin can upload photos for menu items. Veg/non-veg indicators on every item.
🔲
Table Management
Generate and download QR codes per table. Track table occupancy in real time.
